Frequently Asked Questions

What if my child is behind their year group? ▾
No problem — very common. We assess every child individually and teach at the level they are at, not the level we expect them to be at.
Do you follow the National Curriculum? ▾
Yes. All programmes are fully aligned with the current National Curriculum for England. Our GCSE content covers AQA, Edexcel and OCR specifications.
How quickly will we see improvement? ▾
Most parents notice improved confidence within 4–6 sessions. Measurable grade improvements typically become visible within one term.
