The Process

From First Enquiry to First Session

A clear, transparent process — no pressure, no surprises.

1

Book Your Free Assessment

Complete the form on our Contact page or call us directly. Takes under 5 minutes. No payment required.

💡 The assessment is completely free and carries no obligation whatsoever.
2

Initial Evaluation

Your child attends a structured, friendly assessment session. Our qualified teachers evaluate current attainment, identify learning gaps, and understand your child's goals.

📋 Parents receive a verbal summary at the end of the session and a written overview within 48 hours.
3

Personalised Learning Plan Created

We create an individual learning plan tailored to your child's current level, target outcomes, and preferred learning style. You'll receive a copy and have the opportunity to discuss it with us.

4

Weekly Tuition Begins

Sessions are 1 hour 45 minutes. Structured, engaging, curriculum-aligned lessons with homework set and reviewed each session.

📅 Mon–Fri 4pm–6:30pm · Saturday 10am–3:45pm · Sunday 10am–12pm
5

Ongoing Progress Monitoring & Reporting

Regular written progress updates to parents. The learning plan is reviewed each term and adjusted as your child develops.
