11+ Preparation

Grammar School Success Starts Here

Comprehensive 11+ preparation for GL Assessment and CEM exams.

What the 11+ Tests

GL Assessment

Tests: Verbal Reasoning, Non-Verbal Reasoning, Mathematics, English.

CEM (Durham University)

Tests: Verbal, Numerical Reasoning, and Spatial Reasoning. Less predictable format.

Frequently Asked Questions

When should we start 11+ preparation? ▾
Ideally Year 4 for a relaxed approach, or the start of Year 5 for a structured programme. Year 6 is not too late — we run intensive preparation for late starters.
Which grammar schools can you prepare for? ▾
We prepare for all selective schools accessible from Redbridge, including Ilford County High, Woodford County High, Bancroft's School, and others.
Do you offer mock exams? ▾
Yes. Mock exams under timed conditions are a core part of our 11+ programme. We review results with parents and use them to target remaining gaps.
