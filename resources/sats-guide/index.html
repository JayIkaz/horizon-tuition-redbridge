Year 6 SATs: A Parent's Complete Guide

📅 May 2026⏱ 7 min readHorizon Tuition

SATs week can feel daunting for both children and parents. With the right preparation and mindset, the vast majority of children perform at or above their expected level.

What Are the Year 6 SATs?

Year 6 SATs take place in May and assess children against expected standards in English and Maths. They inform secondary school setting and provide a national benchmark of performance.

What Papers Will My Child Sit?

  • English Reading (1 paper, 1 hour)
  • English Grammar, Punctuation and Spelling (2 papers)
  • Maths Arithmetic (1 paper, 30 minutes)
  • Maths Reasoning (2 papers, 40 minutes each)

How to Support Your Child at Home

The most important thing is to keep the atmosphere calm. Encourage regular reading, practise times tables together, and ensure your child gets adequate sleep in the weeks leading up to exams.
