SEN & Dyslexia Support

Specialist Support for Children Who Learn Differently

Our specialist SEN programme uses proven multisensory approaches to help every child succeed.

If your child has been diagnosed with dyslexia, dyscalculia, or ADHD — or if you suspect they may have an unidentified learning difference — our specialist team can help.

Dyslexia

Structured literacy using multisensory techniques

Dyscalculia

Concrete-pictorial-abstract approach to build genuine number understanding

ADHD

Shorter task cycles, movement breaks, and high-engagement activities

Delayed Readers

Phonics, fluency and comprehension at the child's actual reading level

FAQs for SEN Families

Do you need a diagnosis to access SEN support? ▾
No. Many families come to us with concerns but no formal diagnosis. We work with the child regardless.
Do you work with EHCPs? ▾
Yes. We are familiar with EHCPs and can work in alignment with your child's plan. We are happy to liaise with schools where appropriate.
How long before we see progress? ▾
Progress with SEN support is often slower and less linear — but it is real. Many parents notice improved confidence within 6–8 sessions, before academic gains become visible.
