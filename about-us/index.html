Our Story

Built on a Simple Belief: Every Child Deserves Expert Teaching

Horizon Tuition Redbridge was founded to address a genuine gap in the local market — a need for tuition delivered not by university students, but by qualified, experienced teachers who understand child development, curriculum design, and how to genuinely move a child forward.

We are based in Barkingside and serve families across Redbridge, Ilford, Seven Kings, Woodford, and the surrounding area.
